Features

Everything you need to automate email

MailPilot gives you relevance filtering, RAG grounding, and safe automation controls—so your team replies faster without losing quality.

Inbox Intelligence
Automatically reads Gmail and IMAP inboxes, filters noise, and identifies emails that need a response — powered by LLM relevance scoring.
RAG-Powered Replies
Enriches every AI reply with your knowledge base via Postgres + pgvector. Answers are grounded in your documentation and FAQs.
Secure Multi-Tenancy
Per-user isolation — scoped credentials, tenant-prefixed state, and audit logging built in.
Flexible Scheduling
Runs in-process or scales with Celery + Redis. Periodic polling, manual triggers, and queue support.
Gmail OAuth + SMTP/IMAP
Connect via Google OAuth 2.0 or any SMTP/IMAP provider. Token refresh and callback handling included.
Dashboard & Audit Trail
See pending queue, sent replies, skipped emails, and audit logs — scoped to your account.
